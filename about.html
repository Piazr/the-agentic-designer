Applied AI Signals & Systems Builder

I am Mehran Mozaffari. I build and operate applied AI systems that connect models to real workflows, real users, and measurable outcomes.

My work sits at the intersection of:

  • Agentic systems and LLM operations: Re-engineering the design-to-production pipeline around autonomous, multi-agent frameworks.
  • Voice and telephony automation: Bringing conversational intelligence and latency-sensitive context routing to dialer workflows.
  • Workflow orchestration: Closing the loop between models and messaging queues, APIs, and business systems.
  • Practical AI product direction: Delivering honest capability assessments and strategy guides for domain-heavy businesses.

I wrote The Agentic Designer as a hands-on field manual to help designers, engineers, and product builders navigate the shift from static interfaces to autonomous, model-driven agent workflows. This book compiles the patterns, schemas, and systems architecture needed to build consistent, production-grade applications using AI coding agents.
